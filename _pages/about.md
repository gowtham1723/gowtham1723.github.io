I am currently a Ph.D. student in Electrical and Computer Engineering at the University of Maryland, College Park, MD, USA. I received my Bachelors degree in Computer Engineering from University of Jaffna, Sri Lanka, in 2021.  I am currently working with <a href='https://isr.umd.edu/clark/faculty/391/Carol-Espy-Wilson'>Prof.Carol Espy-Wilson</a> as a Graduate Research Assistant in the <a href='https://scl.umd.edu/'>Speech Communication Laboratory</a> in Institue of Systems Research, University of Maryland- College Park.

My research is primarily focused on utilizing deep learning for speech communication based applications . My works combines knowledge of digital signal processing, speech science, natural language processing and machine learning to conduct interdisciplinary research in the detection and monitoring of mental health using multimodal approaches including speech, video and text.
